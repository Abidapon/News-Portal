50 new buses on Ghatarchar-Kanchpur route from Dec 26: Taposh

Fifty new buses – 20 of Trans Sliva and 30 of BRTC – will be launched on the Ghatarchar to Kanchpur via Motijheel and Signboard routes on December 26, said Dhaka South City Corporation Mayor Sheikh Fazle Noor Taposh today. Drivers and helpers of the buses will have uniforms and the drivers will have identity cards on them, said the mayor during a meeting at Nagar Bhaban today.
